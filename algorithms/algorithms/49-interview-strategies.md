# Interview Strategies

## The 45-Minute Timeline

1.  **0-5 mins**: Introductions.
2.  **5-10 mins**: Problem Statement & Clarification.
    *   Restate problem.
    *   Ask constraints.
    *   Create example.
3.  **10-15 mins**: Approach & Discussion.
    *   Propose Brute Force.
    *   Propose Optimal (Pattern).
    *   Agree on approach.
4.  **15-35 mins**: Coding.
    *   Write clean code.
    *   Talk while typing.
5.  **35-40 mins**: Testing & Debugging.
    *   Dry run.
    *   Fix bugs.
6.  **40-45 mins**: Questions for Interviewer.

## Communication Tips
*   **Think Aloud**: Silence is a red flag.
*   **Pivot**: If stuck, say "I'm stuck on X, I'm thinking of trying Y."
*   **Listen**: If interviewer says "Are you sure?", you are likely wrong. Stop and re-evaluate.

## Handling "I Don't Know"
*   Don't panic.
*   Say: "I haven't seen this exact problem, but it reminds me of [Pattern]. Let's try to apply that."
